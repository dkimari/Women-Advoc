About Us


Our advocacy harnesses the most rigorous and compelling evidence to show that investing in girls, women, and gender equality is not only the right thing to do, but also the smart thing to do.

We call for more attention to overlooked topics and partner to generate new evidence.

Moreover, we focus on what works, using advocacy grounded in evidence to shift policies and drive investments.
